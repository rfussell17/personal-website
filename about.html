ABOUT

I’m Robin. A self-taught programmer from Kelowna, BC. I fell in love with coding in 2018 after dabbling in a few small projects and have spent nearly every single day learning or building since then.

I’m a creator at heart. A musician, culinary wizard, gamer, sports enthusiast and now a lifelong student of programming. I started getting my feet wet building a few pages and websites, after I dove into the basics I started creating small web apps and games to further my learning through trial and error. I love programming because I can combine so many of my passions into something real. Learning is something that has always been my biggest passion.

Whether It’s learning an instrument, how to cook challenging dishes, refining my abilities in a sport, or how to dominate strategy games. I've been building a wide array of projects using several different technologies to learn as much as possible in the last 18 months, and I’ve put together my portfolio with projects in mind that include a good range of skills and technologies, to showcase what I have learned.

My skills currently include full-stack web app / software development, JS, HTML, CSS, Java, ReactJS, Bootstrap, jQuery, Github and version control.
